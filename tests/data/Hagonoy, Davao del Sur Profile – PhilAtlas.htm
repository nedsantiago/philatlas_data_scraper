Hagonoy is a coastal municipality in the province of Davao del Sur.

The municipality has a land area of 114.28 square kilometers or 44.12 square miles which constitutes 5.28% of Davao del Sur's total area. Its population as determined by the 2020 Census was 56,919. This represented 8.36% of the total population of Davao del Sur province, or 1.09% of the overall population of the Davao Region. Based on these figures, the population density is computed at 498 inhabitants per square kilometer or 1,290 inhabitants per square mile.
