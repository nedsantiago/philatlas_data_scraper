Bagong Ilog is a barangay in the city of Pasig. Its population as determined by the 2020 Census was 20,344. This represented 2.53% of the total population of Pasig.

Demographics

Households

The household population of Bagong Ilog in the 2015 Census was 16,640 broken down into 4,061 households or an average of 4.10 members per household.

Population by age group

According to the 2015 Census, the age group with the highest population in Bagong Ilog is 25 to 29, with 1,745 individuals. Conversely, the age group with the lowest population is 80 and over, with 134 individuals.

details

Combining age groups together, those aged 14 and below, consisting of the young dependent population which include infants/babies, children and young adolescents/teenagers, make up an aggregate of 24.28% (4,079). Those aged 15 up to 64, roughly, the economically active population and actual or potential members of the work force, constitute a total of 70.57% (11,854). Finally, old dependent population consisting of the senior citizens, those aged 65 and over, total 5.14% (864) in all.

The computed Age Dependency Ratios mean that among the population of Bagong Ilog, there are 34 youth dependents to every 100 of the working age population; there are 7 aged/senior citizens to every 100 of the working population; and overall, there are 42 dependents (young and old-age) to every 100 of the working population.

The median age of 28 indicates that half of the entire population of Bagong Ilog are aged less than 28 and the other half are over the age of 28.

Historical population

The population of Bagong Ilog fell from 22,373 in 1990 to 20,344 in 2020, a decrease of 2,029 people over the course of 30 years. The latest census figures in 2020 denote a positive growth rate of 4.12%, or an increase of 3,547 people, from the previous population of 16,797 in 2015.
