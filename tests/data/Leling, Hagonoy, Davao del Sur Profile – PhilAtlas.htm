Leling is a barangay in the municipality of Hagonoy, in the province of Davao del Sur. Its population as determined by the 2020 Census was 3,178. This represented 5.58% of the total population of Hagonoy.

Demographics

Households

The household population of Leling in the 2015 Census was 2,721 broken down into 716 households or an average of 3.80 members per household.

Population by age group

According to the 2015 Census, the age group with the highest population in Leling is 5 to 9, with 290 individuals. Conversely, the age group with the lowest population is 80 and over, with 13 individuals.

details

Combining age groups together, those aged 14 and below, consisting of the young dependent population which include infants/babies, children and young adolescents/teenagers, make up an aggregate of 30.80% (838). Those aged 15 up to 64, roughly, the economically active population and actual or potential members of the work force, constitute a total of 64.39% (1,752). Finally, old dependent population consisting of the senior citizens, those aged 65 and over, total 4.81% (131) in all.

The computed Age Dependency Ratios mean that among the population of Leling, there are 48 youth dependents to every 100 of the working age population; there are 7 aged/senior citizens to every 100 of the working population; and overall, there are 55 dependents (young and old-age) to every 100 of the working population.

The median age of 26 indicates that half of the entire population of Leling are aged less than 26 and the other half are over the age of 26.

Historical population

The population of Leling grew from 1,727 in 1990 to 3,178 in 2020, an increase of 1,451 people over the course of 30 years. The latest census figures in 2020 denote a positive growth rate of 3.32%, or an increase of 457 people, from the previous population of 2,721 in 2015.
